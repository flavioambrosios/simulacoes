# Referências Sugeridas para Leitura

## 1) Simulações e aprendizagem em Ciências/Física

1. Rutten, N.; van Joolingen, W. R.; van der Veen, J. T. (2012). The learning effects of computer simulations in science education. *Computers & Education*, 58(1), 136-153.
2. Smetana, L. K.; Bell, R. L. (2012). Computer simulations to support science instruction and learning: A critical review of the literature. *International Journal of Science Education*, 34(9), 1337-1370.
3. de Jong, T.; Linn, M. C.; Zacharia, Z. C. (2013). Physical and virtual laboratories in science and engineering education. *Science*, 340(6130), 305-308.
4. Wieman, C. E.; Adams, W. K.; Perkins, K. K. (2008). PhET: Simulations that enhance learning. *Science*, 322(5902), 682-683.

## 2) Design instrucional e aprendizagem multimídia

1. Mayer, R. E. (2009). *Multimedia Learning* (2nd ed.). Cambridge University Press.
2. Clark, R. C.; Mayer, R. E. (2016). *E-Learning and the Science of Instruction* (4th ed.). Wiley.
3. Sweller, J.; van Merriënboer, J. J. G.; Paas, F. (2019). Cognitive Architecture and Instructional Design: 20 years later. *Educational Psychology Review*, 31, 261-292.

## 3) Avaliação formativa e feedback

1. Black, P.; Wiliam, D. (1998). Assessment and classroom learning. *Assessment in Education*, 5(1), 7-74.
2. Hattie, J.; Timperley, H. (2007). The power of feedback. *Review of Educational Research*, 77(1), 81-112.
3. Brookhart, S. M. (2017). *How to Give Effective Feedback to Your Students* (2nd ed.). ASCD.

## 4) Metodologia em ensino de Ciências

1. Creswell, J. W.; Creswell, J. D. (2018). *Research Design* (5th ed.). SAGE.
2. Shadish, W. R.; Cook, T. D.; Campbell, D. T. (2002). *Experimental and Quasi-Experimental Designs for Generalized Causal Inference*. Houghton Mifflin.
3. Flick, U. (2018). *An Introduction to Qualitative Research* (6th ed.). SAGE.

## 5) Referências normativas (Brasil)

1. Brasil. Lei no 13.709/2018 (LGPD).
2. Brasil. Conselho Nacional de Saúde. Resolução CNS no 510/2016.
3. Brasil. Ministério da Educação. Base Nacional Comum Curricular (BNCC).

## Sugestão de uso prático

- Curto prazo: Rutten (2012), Smetana & Bell (2012), Black & Wiliam (1998), LGPD.
- Médio prazo: de Jong et al. (2013), Mayer (2009), Hattie & Timperley (2007), Creswell (2018).
- Longo prazo: ampliar para desenho quase-experimental e metanálises recentes.
